Pilgrims Kitchen & Inn ຍິນດີຕ້ອນຮັບ

Nestle by the banks of the Mekong River with sweeping views across to Thailand, we bring together a cozy cafe, a vibrant multi-cuisine restaurant, a welcoming guesthouse, and our very own in-house coffee roastery - all under one roof. Our cafe is the perfect place to begin your day with a fresh cup of coffee while watching the river awaken, while our restaurant takes you on a journey of flavors from around the world - Mexican, Indian, American, Mediterranean, and Local Lao specialties.

For travelers, our guesthouse offers the comfort of 11 well-appointed rooms and 2 spacious suites, where you can rest, recharge, and wake up to the beauty of the Mekong sunrise. And for coffee lovers, our in-house roasting ensures every cup is crafted with passion, freshness, and care. Whether you are passing through, staying the night, or simply looking for a place to relax and connect, we invite you to discover comfort, flavor, and unforgettable riverside experiences with us.

Our Story

ເມນູຂອງພວກເຮົາ

ສຳຫຼວດເມນູອາຫານຫຼາກຫຼາຍຈາກທົ່ວໂລກ ທີ່ປຸງແຕ່ງດ້ວຍຄວາມຕັ້ງໃຈແລະວັດຖຸດິບຊັ້ນດີ

ເລື່ອນເບິ່ງເມນູເພີ່ມເຕີມ

Riverside Experience

Our riverside retreat offers more than just a place to stay — it’s a complete experience by the banks of the Mekong, with sweeping views of Thailand. From freshly roasted coffee at our café to global flavors at our multi-cuisine restaurant, every detail is designed to delight your senses.

Whether you’re here for a hearty breakfast, a leisurely riverside lunch, or a romantic sunset dinner, our team is dedicated to making every moment memorable. Paired with the comfort of our guesthouse and the aroma of coffee roasted in-house, your time with us is crafted to be both relaxing and unforgettable.
